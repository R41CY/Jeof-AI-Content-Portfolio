## 🛠️ AI Tools & Technologies Used
The videos and content showcased in this portfolio were meticulously crafted using the following state-of-the-art tools:
- **Prompt Engineering**: Crafting precise, detailed instructions to guide generative models.
- **ChatGPT & Claude**: Ideation, scriptwriting, and refining complex prompts.
- **Nano Banana**: High-fidelity AI image generation for storyboarding and visual assets.
- **Google Labs (Veo 3.1)**: Advanced AI video generation, turning still images and prompts into photorealistic cinematic motion.
- **CapCut**: Since AI video generators often limit clips to ~8 seconds, CapCut is used to seamlessly combine sequences, edit flow, add precision captions, and integrate audio for a polished final product.

## 💡 About The Content
The portfolio features two main categories:
1. **Educational Explainers**: Breaking down complex topics (e.g., health, science) into engaging, easy-to-understand visual narratives.
2. **Cinematic AI Showcases**: Pushing the boundaries of what's possible in digital storytelling by blurring the lines between real-world footage and AI-generated content.

*Note: During the learning and training process, several popular video formats and content styles were replicated to deeply hone skills in AI generation and storytelling.*
