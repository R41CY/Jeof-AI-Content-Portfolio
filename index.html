About

The Creator

I'm Jeof — an AI content creator based in Metro Manila, Philippines, passionate about blending artificial intelligence with visual storytelling. From educational explainers that break down complex health topics to photorealistic cinematic pieces that challenge the line between real and AI-generated, my work explores what's possible when creativity meets cutting-edge technology.

Each video is meticulously crafted using state-of-the-art AI generation tools. Since models like Veo are often limited to 8 seconds or less per clip, I use CapCut to seamlessly combine sequences, add precise captions, and edit everything together to achieve the highest level of visual fidelity and narrative impact. During my learning and training process, I have also replicated several popular video contents to deeply hone my skills in AI generation and storytelling.
